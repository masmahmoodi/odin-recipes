Quabuli

Discription

Qabuli: A classic Afghan rice dish that is a true delight for the taste buds. Qabuli features aromatic and flavorful basmati rice cooked with succulent pieces of tender slow-cooked meat, such as lamb or chicken.

Ingredients

  • Basmati rice
  • Meat
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Rinse and soak the rice
  2. Prepare the meat
  3. Slow-cook the meat
