Yakhni

Discription

Yakhni is a flavorful and aromatic broth that holds a special place in Afghan and South Asian cuisine. It is a key element used to create rich soups, stews, and meat-based curries. Yakhni is known for its delicate balance of spices, herbs, and slow-cooked meat, resulting in a hearty and comforting dish.
