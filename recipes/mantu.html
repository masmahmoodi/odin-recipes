Mantu

Discription

Afghani Mantu: Savory, steamed dumplings filled with seasoned meat, served with a flavorful tomato-based sauce. A favorite in Afghan cuisine.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef or lamb
  • Onion
  • Ginger
  • Ground cumin

Steps

  1. Prepare the Meat Filling: - In a bowl, combine ground beef or lamb with finely chopped onion, minced garlic, grated ginger, salt, pepper, ground coriander, cumin, and cinnamon. - Mix the ingredients thoroughly until well combined.
  2. Assemble the Dumplings: - Take a dumpling or wonton wrapper and spoon a small amount of the meat filling onto the center. - Moisten the edges of the wrapper with water to help with sealing. - Fold the wrapper in half to create a triangle, then pinch and seal the edges together. You can also fold the corners to create a more elaborate shape.
  3. Steam the Dumplings: - Arrange the filled dumplings in a steamer basket lined with parchment paper or cabbage leaves to prevent sticking. - Place the steamer basket over a pot of boiling water, ensuring the water does not touch the dumplings. - Steam the dumplings for about 20-25 minutes, or until cooked through and tender.
